Connect With Me

Mukesh Mohan Gupta

President, Chamber of Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (CIMSME)

India’s Leading MSME Voice | President, Chamber of Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises | Directorship Dena Bank, Hindustan Fertilizers Corporation Limited, Madras Fertilizers Limited & a TATA Subsidiary I Author — MSMEs of Developed India – an Encyclopaedia of MSME Schemes | PM’s Official Invitee on MSME Policy | Helping 8 Cr + MSMEs Grow

For more than 30 years, I have worked with one single mission. To ensure that every MSME in India gets the finance, policy support, timely payments and awareness it deserves.

As President of the Chamber of Indian Micro Small and Medium Enterprises (CIMSME), I have had the privilege of being personally invited by Hon'ble Prime Minister Sh. Narendra Modi Ji at his official residence to discuss MSME issues, as one of only seven dignitaries in that meeting.

I have authored two books, "MSMEs of Developed India - An Encyclopaedia of MSME Schemes" and "MSME Digest" Published by Bharat Publications and Taxman Publication.

I hosted many senior Ministers, RBI Deputy Governors, and banking leaders on my national TV show "Positive Talk with Mukesh Mohan Gupta" - on Zee Business.

I built MSME Helpline, a digital platform connecting MSME entrepreneurs with loans, solutions of delayed payments, schemes, and expert guidance.

Today, through CIMSME and MSME Helpline, I work on the ground to help MSME owners navigate government schemes, access collateral-free finance, resolve delayed payments, and grow sustainably. Whether it is policy advocacy, awareness programs, or direct consulting, my focus is always on the 8 crore+ MSMEs that form the backbone of India's economy.

I believe deeply in the vision of Aatmanirbhar Bharat and work every day to ensure that the government's schemes and support actually reach the entrepreneurs they are designed for.

I have created a course to develop Para-Professionals who will assist MSMEs mainly at Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities at affordable costs, as was proposed in the financial budget 2026-27 at MSME Mitra Course .

MSME Health Report to be launched on MSME International Day to empower the MSMEs befor they apply for any bank loan.
